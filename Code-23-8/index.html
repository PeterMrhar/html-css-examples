Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper

Steps:

  1. Beat the eggs in a bowl.
  2. Melt butter in a pan.
  3. Add eggs and stir until cooked.

Simple Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 tomato
  • 1 cucumber
  • Olive oil and vinegar

Steps:

  1. Chop the vegetables.
  2. Drizzle with oil and vinegar.
  3. Toss and serve.
