No-Yeast Bread - Simple Recipe

Ingredients for No-Yeast Bread

  • 500 g flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 350 ml warm water

How to Make No-Yeast Bread

First, mix all the dry ingredients together. Add the vinegar and warm water and knead until the dough is smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased baking pan and bake at 200°C for 30-35 minutes or until the crust is golden brown.
